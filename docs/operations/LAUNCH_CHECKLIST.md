# DIREKT Launch Checklist

## Product
- MVP loop passes;
- pilot findings resolved/accepted;
- copy and trust limitations approved;
- categories/areas populated;
- support content current.

## Android
- signed AAB;
- target API/current policy;
- device/accessibility/performance;
- data safety/store assets;
- staged rollout.

## Backend/data
- production environment;
- migrations;
- backups/restore;
- private storage;
- rate limits;
- jobs/queues;
- API docs.

## Trust/operations
- reviewer/field staffing;
- evidence requirements;
- calibration;
- expiry;
- complaints/incidents/appeals;
- audit.

## Security/privacy/legal
- threat/security review;
- secrets/access;
- privacy/terms/provider agreement;
- Zambia counsel sign-off;
- retention/deletion;
- incident response.

## Commercial
- prices/entitlements;
- payment/reconciliation;
- receipts/refunds;
- support scripts;
- no pay-to-verify language.

## Go/no-go
Named approvers, known risks, stop criteria, rollback and monitoring window.
