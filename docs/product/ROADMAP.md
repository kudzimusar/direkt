# DIREKT Roadmap

Dates are not promised until staffing and Phase 1 research are complete. Roadmap is sequence-based.

## Horizon 0 — Planning baseline

- documentation and source-of-truth controls;
- Pages and documentation CI;
- one sequential build branch.

## Horizon 1 — Validate

- Zambia field research;
- pilot geography/categories;
- evidence and issuing-body matrix;
- prototype and trust comprehension;
- vendor/cost decisions.

## Horizon 2 — Foundation

- Android/backend/admin scaffolding;
- environments and CI;
- authentication, provider/category model;
- audit and storage.

## Horizon 3 — Trust core

- verification case engine;
- field workflow;
- expiry/revocation;
- secure evidence viewer;
- public claim derivation.

## Horizon 4 — Marketplace loop

- customer discovery;
- provider onboarding;
- enquiries;
- reviews and complaints;
- notification centre.

## Horizon 5 — Commercial and operational readiness

- subscriptions/payment;
- reconciliation;
- support tooling;
- analytics;
- security hardening;
- backup/restore.

## Horizon 6 — Zambia pilot

- controlled supply onboarding;
- closed customer test;
- daily operations;
- economics and trust measurement;
- stop/go review.

## Horizon 7 — Android launch

- Play closed/open testing;
- staged production rollout;
- launch operations;
- stabilization.

## Horizon 8 — Evidence-led expansion

Possible after review:

- more Zambian geographies/categories;
- institutional tools;
- provider workforce/member features;
- richer booking or messaging;
- deeper registry/credential integrations.

## Horizon 9 — iOS gate

Native iOS planning begins only after the explicit decision gate in `MASTER_BUILD_PLAN.md`.
