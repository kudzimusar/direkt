# DIREKT Consent and Policy Versioning

## Records

Store policy ID, version, language, effective date, user, accepted/declined time, method, purpose and withdrawal where applicable.

## Separate choices

- terms acceptance;
- privacy acknowledgement/consent where required;
- public premises/location publication;
- contact sharing per enquiry;
- optional marketing;
- analytics choices where required;
- provider declarations;
- evidence/field-visit consent.

Bundling optional marketing with service access is prohibited.

## Changes

Material changes trigger notice and re-acceptance where legally required. Historical acceptance remains auditable. Backend enforces minimum required versions for affected actions.

## Copy

Plain language summary plus accessible full policy. Withdrawal explains functional consequences without blocking unrelated rights.
